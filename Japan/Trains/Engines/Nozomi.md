The Nozomi is the fastest  [[Shinkansen]] on the [[Tokaido Line]] between [[Tokyo]] and [[Kyoto]].

## JRP Coverage
---
The [[Japan Rail Pass]] does not cover Nozomi rides.

## Mechanical Details
---
The Nozomi can travel up to 186 miles per hour.
